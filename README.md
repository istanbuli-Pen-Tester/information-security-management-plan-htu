# Information Security Management Plan – HTU

**Enterprise-level Information Security Management (ISM) plan developed using COBIT 2019, NIST CSF 2.0, and ISO/IEC 27002:2022.**

---

## Overview
This project presents a comprehensive ISM plan for **Istanbuli Auto Gallery**, a luxury automotive business transitioning to a digital-first operating model.

The plan aligns cybersecurity governance with business strategy, risk appetite, and operational priorities while maintaining regulatory and international standards compliance.

---

## Frameworks Used
- **COBIT 2019** – Governance & management objectives, design factors, capability assessment  
- **NIST Cybersecurity Framework 2.0** – Govern, Identify, Protect, Detect, Respond, Recover  
- **ISO/IEC 27002:2022** – Mapping technical and organizational security controls  

---

## Key Deliverables
- Enterprise security vision aligned with business goals
- COBIT design-factor analysis tailored to SME / “Factory IT” role
- Capability maturity assessment (APO13, DSS02)
- 3-year phased security roadmap:
  - **Year 1:** Foundation & risk stabilization  
  - **Year 2:** Repeatable & compliant controls  
  - **Year 3:** Adaptive security posture
- NIST Tier-based current vs target profiles
- Control selection with real-world vendor options
- Cost-aware and risk-prioritized implementation strategy

---

## Core Focus Areas
- Information Security Governance
- Risk Management & Risk Appetite
- Identity & Access Management (IAM)
- Secure Remote Access (VPN + MFA)
- Incident & Change Management
- Compliance & Audit Readiness
